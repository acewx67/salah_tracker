Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Salah Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:
We do not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number.

Prayer Data:
Prayer tracking data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Location Data:
If enabled, the App may access your device location to calculate accurate prayer times. This data is used only on your device and is not stored or shared.

Advertising Data (If Ads Are Enabled):
The App may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob, which may collect device information for personalized or non-personalized ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • To calculate prayer times based on location
  • To store your prayer tracking history locally
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

If advertising services are used, third-party providers may collect certain device data as described in their privacy policies.

4. Data Security

All prayer tracking data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain external databases containing user information.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
