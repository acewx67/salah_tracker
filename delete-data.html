Data Deletion Request - Salah Tracker

This page explains how users of Salah Tracker can delete their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Salah Tracker app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

What Data Is Deleted

  • Account information
  • Prayer logs
  • User preferences

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for legal or fraud-prevention purposes, after which it is permanently deleted.
